Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

World Cup 2026 Odds ("the app", "we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is processed.

Information we collect

The app itself does not require an account and does not collect personal information you provide. Your selected favourite team is stored only on your device.

Advertising

The app shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (including the Identifier for Advertisers, IDFA, only if you allow tracking via the App Tracking Transparency prompt), approximate usage and diagnostic data to serve and measure ads. See Google's policy: policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email, contacts, precise location, or browsing history.

Children

The app is rated 17+ and is not directed to children.

Data retention

We hold no personal data on our servers. Match prediction data is anonymous and public.
