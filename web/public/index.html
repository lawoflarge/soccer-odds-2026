Soccer Odds 2026 is a free iPhone app that shows the betting-market consensus for every match of the 2026 tournament: win/draw/win probabilities, the most likely scoreline, and goal markets like over/under 2.5 and both teams to score, all derived from aggregated bookmaker odds with the margin removed.

How it works

  • For every match, it aggregates the odds from multiple bookmakers and removes the built-in bookmaker margin.
  • The de-margined odds become fair win/draw/win probabilities plus a statistical goal model.
  • Each match shows the most likely scoreline and goal markets: over/under 2.5 goals and both teams to score.
  • Pick a favorite team and follow its predicted path through the tournament.

The result is the market consensus — what the betting market, in aggregate, expects to happen. Predictions reflect the market and are not guaranteed.

For every match, Soccer Odds 2026 collects the odds several bookmakers are offering, removes the built-in bookmaker margin, and averages them into a fair market consensus.
